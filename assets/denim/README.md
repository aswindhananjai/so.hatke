# Denim Canvas Images

Place your denim jacket images here following this naming convention:

## Naming Format
`{brand}-{color}.jpg`

## Examples
- `levis-light.jpg` - Levi's Light Wash
- `levis-dark.jpg` - Levi's Dark Wash
- `levis-black.jpg` - Levi's Black
- `levis-ombre.jpg` - Levi's Custom Ombre
- `zara-light.jpg` - Zara Light Wash
- `hm-black.jpg` - H&M Black
- `thrifted-dark.jpg` - Thrifted Dark Wash

## Image Specifications
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 800x800px (1:1 aspect ratio recommended)
- **View**: Front view, centered
- **Background**: White or transparent
- **Quality**: High resolution

## Required Images
For each brand in `data/denim-canvas.csv`, you need 4 images (one for each color):
- Brand + light
- Brand + dark
- Brand + black
- Brand + ombre
